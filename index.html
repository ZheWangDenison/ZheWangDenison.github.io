I'm an assistant professor of Statistics in the Data Analytics Program at Denison University. I received my Ph.D. in statistics at University of Connecticut in 2020 (Dissertation Topic: "Sequential Estimation Methodologies with Observations Gathered in Groups: Theory, Practice and Data Analysis". Advisors: Dr. Nitis Mukhopadhyay , Dr. Joseph Glaz and Dr. Haim Bar ). I finished my bachelor degree in Statistics in 2016, at Beijing Normal University .

My research interests include applied probability and sequential analysis. I am also interested in multivariate analysis and statistical modeling for time series.
